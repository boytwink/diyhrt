Noire Labs

Noire Labs is a small team producing a few forms of Liquid Estradiol. They've been operating since mid 2022 and are slowly garnering a positive reputation within the Trans DIY community. While we do not know their credentials we know they're a small group of trans people doing this with a reliable well documented process and they provide ample information, qualifications and medical warnings going above and beyond to make sure their customers are well informed. Their product is also extremely cheap and their delivery is quite fast. They are also one of the few providers creating pen cartridges.
